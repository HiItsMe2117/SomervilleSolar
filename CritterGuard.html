Critter Guard





Why it matters:

There are many reasons to get Critter Guard Installed. Long story short; it prevents possible shorts that can turn into fires if the system is not properly grounded.

Our Culprits:

Squirrels are a major culprit in arrays shorting from frayed wiring and possibly causing fires. Pigeons are definitely a runner up and even raccoons have been known to go under panels to avoid the sun in the summer and to stay warm in the winter.
