Our Cleaning Options





Stage 1: Surface Debris

With Stage 1, we use an extendable window cleaning squeegee to clean the panel's surface of any debris such as pollen, dirt and other build-ups that can restrict the light going to your panels. Within this, we will also clear any snow accumulation as well.




Stage 2: Surface and Underneath Debris

Now, with Stage 2 we will lift your panels where there is any major debris like nests and build up of leaves, branches etc. and remove them preventing fires. We will then finish it off with Stage 1, cleaning any surface debris with an extendable window cleaning squeegee.




Stage 3: Surface and Underneath Debris + Pressure Washing

Stage 3 is the complete cleaning! This is to deep clean not only to provide better panel production with Stage 1 or to prevent fires with Stage 2 but to take it a step further and combine them with power washing the roof under the panels. This will reduce any build up molds of other decaying process to your shingles and roof for purpose of extending your roofs lifespan!
